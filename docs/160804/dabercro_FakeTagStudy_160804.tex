  Now that I have a setup that JMAR is happy with for W-scale factors,
  I started the scale factor for fake rates.

  \vspace{12pt}

  First, I made a di-lepton selection (which is mostly muons),
  but that was a bad idea because of lots of di-boson.
  Then, I made a $\gamma$ + jets selection.

\begin{frame}
  \frametitle{Selection}
  Both
  \begin{itemize}
  \item Veto loose b-jets (csv $> 0.605$)
  \item Recoil $> \SI{250}{GeV}$
  \item Fat jet $p_T > \SI{250}{GeV}$
  \item Veto $\tau$
  \end{itemize}
  $\mu\mu$
  \begin{itemize}
  \item Two medium $\mu$, with one also tight
  \item Veto $\gamma$ or additional leptons
  \item $\mu\mu$ mass between 61 and \SI{121}{GeV}
  \end{itemize}
  $\gamma + jets$
  \begin{itemize}
  \item One medium photon ($p_T > \SI{175}{GeV}$)
  \item Veto leptons or additional $\gamma$
  \end{itemize}
\end{frame}

  \frametitle{Conclusions}

  \begin{itemize}
  \item $\mu\mu$ might not be as good to use because of di-boson contamination
  \item $\mu\mu$ also has worse agreement between data and MC
  \item The two scale factors do not match, which is bad
